Difficulty:

Solve the cube in only a few steps!

  1. White Cross

    2. The step of creating the yellow cross can be skipped by directly creating the white cross. The white cross does not require any algorithm

    You have to add in white edge pieces into the white side of the cube and create a cross on the white side.

    Once you are done with adding your white pieces your cube should look somewhat like this.

  2. First Layer

    3. To finish the lowest layer/white face of the cube you have to memorise 4 moves

    R U R' U'

    Align the required piece above where you want to place and repeat the these 4 moves until you it is in place

  3. Second Layer

    4. Align the edge pieces above their colors, forming an upside down T

    If the respective corner is on the right of the edge piece perform

    U R U R' U' F U' F'

    If the respective corner piece is on the left of the edge piece perform

    U' L' U' L U F' U F
